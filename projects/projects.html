General Project Description:

Tagline:

employD, based on the Tinder and Bumble dating application design, is our semester long HCI project. This project is a way to bridge the gap between employers and job applicants during the job search process, by optimizing the job search experience with proven models. We hope that the process, at the end of the project, will result in a more effective and satisfying job search experience for everybody

Summary:

We use human-interaction models and Gestalt Principles and UI Design Principles to create an application to help enhance the job search experience for both job employers and applicants.
